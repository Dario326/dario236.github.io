How To Play:

  1. Click play now from the home page, users can play individually or in groups.
  2. Click players to be prompted to enter name of your team
  3. Team must pick a player to play paper, sissor, rock to figure out who is going to play first
  4. Team must pick a category and a dollar amount then click that box for a question will appear
  5. Students must give the answer in the form of a question before clicking the question again
  6. To see if a student or group is correct, click again for the answer.
  7. At the bottom of the screen you can at a point to the correct team
