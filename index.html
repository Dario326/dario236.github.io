Basic info about Basketball

Mental Minded Basketball in Atlanta, GA

The mission for Mental Minded Academy is to develop mental skill to the young athletes as well as their physical and fundamental skill. Teaching kids basic mental skill before any other skill is what the organization want to focus on to create intelligent in their minds. In doing that, the training will focus on Knowledge, Fundamentals, Strength, and Agility. The brand will further be great in the future with the help from the community if we can keep the kids active. Furthermore, it will open up opportunities for other kids in the community who wants to improve their skill ability. The brand will be more than just a training in the future. It will become a life style in citities in the state for kids starting in elementary-school, to middle school, to high school or college. Basketball is similar to our daily life when it comes to communication, time management, overcoming obstacles, making sacrifices, dealing with success and failure, and taking charge.
